Who am I?

Hi, my name is Mohamed Saad IBN SEDDIK , I'm an engineer and currently a Ph.D. candidate . I received my engineering degree in Embedded Systems & Robotics in 2012 from the ENSTA Bretagne in Brest, FRANCE. During the same year, I remotely followed a Master Degree program from the University of Angers and succesfully obtained the degree with majors in Signal Processing & Dynamic Systems .

After graduation, I worked as a research engineer for CGG's Corporate Technology Office for three months. Then I got the opportunity to start a Ph.D. in Underwater Robotics from January 2013 to December 2015 funded by CGG .

Under Pr. Luc JAULIN supervision, my work is related to Underwater Multi-Robot Localization using Set-Membership Methods , with results exploitable for the Oil & Gas industry and specially for CGG .
